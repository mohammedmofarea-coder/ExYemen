About Yemen

Yemen, located at the southern end of the Arabian Peninsula, is a land of ancient history, stunning architecture, and diverse landscapes. From the unique biodiversity of Socotra Island to the ancient skyscrapers of Shibam, Yemen offers unforgettable experiences for every traveler.

With a history dating back over 3,000 years, Yemen is home to some of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world. Its rich cultural heritage is reflected in its architecture, cuisine, and traditional crafts.
